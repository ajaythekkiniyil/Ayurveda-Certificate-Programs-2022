About

Panchakarma Therapy

The Panchakarma therapy course enables the candidates to get hands on training in various traditional panchakarma therapies. Also, they will be proficient in executing Keraleeya panchakarma therapies & practices.

Scope of the Course
  • To be an expert in performing Panchakarma therapies

Ayurvedic Pharmacy

Students once completing the course are capable of meeting different requirements of both manufacturing and consumer sector of Ayurvedic drugs. For manufacturing sector, they will be in a position to take care of classical methods of drug manufacturing, drug selection, standardization, quality control, drug store management and other manufacturing requirements. For consuming sector, they will be qualified persons for drug dispensing and other related activities. Also, the course aims to produce qualified technologist who have the capacity of producing high quality Ayurvedic drugs by employing traditional techniques and also to control and maintain the quality.

Scope of the Course
  • To be an expert in manufacturing of Ayurvedic formulations.
  • To identify, collect and process the raw materials used in Ayurvedic drugs, and formulations.
  • To be conversant with drugs and cosmetics act and regulations.

Ayurvedic Nursing

Students once completing the course are capable of meeting different requirements in nursing care in different Ayurveda specialties. They will be proficient enough to provide nursing care at the level of outpatient & inpatient departments. At the OP level they may be able to assist the physician in partaking the OP related practices & procedures. They also will be competent in performing different inpatient ward practices & procedures and also in assisting the para surgical procedures in minor OT along with dispensing of Ayurvedic medicines.

Scope of the Course
  • To be an expert in assisting physicians in the OP
  • To be an expert in Ayurvedic specialty nursing care
